01 About

I have been a part of the Levi's team since 2018, based in their luxury made-to-order department Lot No. 1. Here I work alongside other tailors to design, pattern draft and manufacture jeans. Lot No. 1 jeans are completely customisable through a one-on-one design experience, from the type of denim to the colour of the rivets. Currently only available in five cities around the world — London, Paris, Tokyo, New York and San Francisco — there is no other Levi's® experience like it.
