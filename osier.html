02 My Journey

My journey at OSIER began at the end of my international exchange program and continued to be an enriching and rewarding journey to help the company grow and expand rapidly to international markets over the span on two-years.
Alongside CEO Theresa Jentzsch, I informed the creative direction of the brand, fabric sourcing, sustainable focus, hardware design/manufacture and communications with manufactures and suppliers. Other responsibilities included technical specification packs, fabric sourcing, quality assurance checks with our factory, and general creative direction of the brand.
At Osier all of our products are designed and hand manufactured in Europe with the finest vegan materials. We are constantly exploring innovative ways to remain ethical, sustainable and eco-friendly as our values care for both our animals and our planet as we pave the footsteps towards our future. We launched our first capsule collection in August 2018 at The Impact Shop, Amsterdam.
